My Hero Acadmia

My Hero Academia (僕ぼくのヒーローアカデミア Boku no Hīrō Akademia?) is an anime series produced by Toho and Bones based on the manga by Kohei Horikoshi. It was announced in Issue 49, 2015 of Weekly Shonen Jump. On March 8, 2016, Funimation announced it had acquired exclusive international rights to the series. MBS co-produced the first season.

Jojo's Bizzare Adventure
